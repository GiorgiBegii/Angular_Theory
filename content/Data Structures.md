### LIFO (Last In, First Out)
**LIFO** means: the last thing you put in is the first thing you take out.**

<span style="color:#00ff41;">Think about a stack of plates:</span>
- Put plate A
- Put plate B
- Put plate C

Top
- C  ← last plate added
- B
- A

<span style="color:#00ff41;">If you take a plate, you get C first:</span>
- Take out C  
- Take out B  
- Take out A

So:

- Last in → **C**
- First out → **C**

That's why it's called **Last In, First Out (LIFO)**.

### FIFO (First In, First Out)
**FIFO means: the first thing you put in is the first thing you take out.**

<span style="color:#00ff41;">Think about people standing in a queue:</span>
A → B → C

- A arrived first
- B arrived second
- C arrived third


<span style="color:#00ff41;">When serving people:</span>
- A leaves first  
- Then B  
- Then C

So:

- First in → **A**
- First out → **A**

That's why it's called **First In, First Out (FIFO)**.
